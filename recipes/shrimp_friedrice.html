Shrimp Fried Rice

Description

A simple dish that will take less than 40 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of water
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of uncooked white rice
  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup of fresh bean sprouts
  • 1/2 cup of chopped onion
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of cooked medium shrimp that is peeled and deveined without tail
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onion
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Bring water to boil in a saucepan. Add rice and stir. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat large wok for 2 minutes. Once wok is hot, pour in vegetable oil, beansprouts, and onions. Mix well and cook for 3 minutes.
  3. Mix in rice and shrimp and cook for 3 minutes stirring constantly.
  4. Add in eggs, green onion, salt, black pepper and cook for 4 minutes stirring continuously unlit eggs are cooked.
