Baked Chicken

Description

A simple dish that will take less than 40 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • 6 skin-on and bone-in chicken thighs
  • Dry-rub seasoning of your choice

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F and place aluminum foil in baking pan.
  2. Rub seasoning onto chicken and place it in the baking pan.
  3. Bake chicken for 40 minutes in preheated oven.
  4. Let chicken cool for 5 minutes before serving
